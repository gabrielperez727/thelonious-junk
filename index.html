Featured Article

How Islam Saved Western Civilization

History

Dr. Roy Casagranda the college professor you always wished you had. His lecture here is a great introduction to the often ignored and dismissed impact thinkers from the 'Middle East' have had on Western civilization. Al-Khwarizmi’s algebra, Ibn Sina’s work on medicine, Ibn al-Haytham’s optics, Ibn Khaldun’s corpus of works, and even Persian King Cyrus the Great’s declaration of human rights all highlight the immense influence this region has had.
