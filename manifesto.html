    Cultivating Curiosity

    Thelonious Junk Manifesto

    Thelonious Junk is to serve as a sanctuary for the curious soul, a curation of interesting things. Here, you will encounter intriguing individuals, view inspiring films, listen to diverse music, and engage with the foreign, the unconventional, the discarded and forgotten voices, and the unorthodox perspectives.

    Every encounter is an opportunity—an invitation, really—to experience something new and fresh, expand your mind, challenge your beliefs and preconceptions, and awaken your senses to the richness and complexity of life, alternate perspectives, and exposure to the unconventional ways of the world.

    It's simple. It's a place where the curious can find what they seek. And maybe, just maybe, find a little different view of the world.
